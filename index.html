About the Program

What is a Growth Lab?

Growth Lab is a digital education program run on Slack, where we bring multiple companies together in a shared channel — or run exclusively for a single enterprise account. Over a defined period (typically two weeks), participants receive one short video per day, each paired with step-by-step guidance they can follow directly inside Amplitude. The results are measurable: Growth Lab participants have increased Amplitude usage by ~25–26% during the program, with teams reporting new use cases tied to activation, retention, and experimentation.
